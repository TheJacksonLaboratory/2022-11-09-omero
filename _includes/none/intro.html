OMERO is a modern client-server software platform for visualizing, managing, and annotating scientific image data. OMERO lets you import and archive your images, annotate and tag them, record your experimental protocols, and export images in a number of formats. It also allows you to collaborate with colleagues anywhere in the world by creating user groups with different permission levels. This workshop will teach you to view and annotate data, work with regions of interest, analyze OMERO data with ImageJ, and create figures. The specific contents will be tailored to the participants, so that they can use the knowledge to their own research problems.
